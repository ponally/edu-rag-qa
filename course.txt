英语口语课程 - 餐厅场景教学资料

【点餐常用表达】
1. Can I take your order? 我可以点餐了吗？
2. I'd like a cup of coffee. 我想要一杯咖啡。
3. What do you recommend? 你有什么推荐？
4. How would you like your steak? 你的牛排要几分熟？
5. The bill, please. 请买单。
6. Could I see the menu, please? 可以给我看一下菜单吗？
7. I'll have the same. 我要和他一样的。
8. Is this dish spicy? 这道菜辣吗？
9. I'm allergic to nuts. 我对坚果过敏。
10. Can I get this to go? 我可以打包吗？

【餐厅场景词汇】
menu 菜单, appetizer 开胃菜, main course 主菜, dessert 甜点
reservation 预订, waiter 服务员, tip 小费
chef 厨师, buffet 自助餐, set meal 套餐
rare 一分熟, medium 五分熟, well-done 全熟

【常见语法点】
- "I'd like..." 是礼貌点餐的标准句型，比"I want"更正式
- "Could I..." 比"Can I..."更礼貌，适合正式餐厅
- 表达过敏/饮食限制时用"I'm allergic to..."或"I don't eat..."

【对话示例】
场景：顾客进入餐厅
Waiter: Good evening! Do you have a reservation?
Customer: No, I don't. Do you have a table for two?
Waiter: Of course! Right this way.

场景：点餐
Waiter: Are you ready to order?
Customer: Yes. I'd like the grilled salmon, please.
Waiter: How would you like it cooked?
Customer: Medium, please.

场景：结账
Customer: Excuse me, could we have the bill, please?
Waiter: Of course. Here you are.
Customer: Can I pay by credit card?
Waiter: Certainly!
